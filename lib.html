Library


Administrative In-Charge: Dr. Sanjay Gupta

Office Phone: 01672-253506

The Central Library is housed in a modern building having all kinds of facilities for its best utilization by the student community. It has large number of volume of technical books along with a good collection of books on literature, general awareness, management and moral sciences etc. The central library is already subscribing to a large number of National and International journals. Besides print subscription users are having access to full text of articles from journals under e-Shodhsindhu Consortium. The central library is having all the modern facilities like Plasma TV; MMP; CTV; OHP; Slide Projector etc.The library is equipped with 30 desktop Computers in Digital Section to enable the library users to access all the subscribed e- resources. Efforts have been made so that users may feel comfort while they study or consult library material for their study and research purpose.
