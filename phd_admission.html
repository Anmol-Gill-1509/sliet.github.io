Ph.D Admissions


The award of the Ph.D. Degree is in respect of high achievements, independent research and application of scientific knowledge to the solution of scientific and technical problems. The admission to Ph.D. programme (Part Time/Full Time) will be strictly as per institute rules.

ELIGIBILITY:

    1. Engineering & Technology (Except Food Engineering & Technology): B.E./B.Tech. and M.E./ M.Tech. OR Integrated Master’s Degree in Engineering/Technology degree in relevant branch OR equivalent degree with First Class in any one of the Degrees.
    2. Food Engineering & Technology: Bachelor’s degree in Science/Engineering/Technology and Master’s Degree in Food Science/Technology/Engineering OR Microbial & Food Technology, OR Biotechnology OR Integrated Master’s Degree in Engineering /Technology OR equivalent degree with First Class in any one of the Degrees.
  2. Sciences: Bachelor’s degree in Science/Technology/Engineering and Master’s Degree in Science/ Technology/ Engineering in relevant discipline OR Integrated Master’s Degree in Science/ Technology/Engineering OR equivalent degree with First Class in any one of the Degrees.
  3. Humanities: Bachelor’s Degree and Master’s Degree in Humanities in relevant discipline OR equivalent degree with First Class in any of the Degrees.
  4. Management: Bachelor’s Degree and Master’s Degree in Management/Engineering in relevant discipline OR equivalent degree with First Class in any of the Degrees.

Seats:

  1. Ph.D. Fellowship sanctioned by Ministry of Education = 118 (Maximum)
  2. AICTE Doctoral Fellowship (ADF) = 08
  3. Ph.D. seats funded by various agencies like UGC, CSIR are filled as per their sanction and slot available with Faculty.
  4. Full Time without fellowship and Part Time Ph.D. seats are also filled as per applications and slot available with Faculty.
